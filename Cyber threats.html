CYBER THREATS

A cyber or cybersecurity threat is a malicious act that seeks to damage data, steal data, or disrupt digital life in general. In other words, it is the easiest way to steal sensitive information, disrupt operations, or spread misinformation.

These threats can come in different forms,

Social engineering

The most common among people is when individuals are manipulated into divulging confidential information such as bank account PINs or other passcodes.

Phishing

The usage of deceptive emails, messages, or websites designed to trick users into sharing sensitive information.

Malware

Malicious software that infiltrates a system to cause damage or gain unauthorized access.

for example-
  • viruses
  • worms
  • trojan horse
  • spyware
  • ransomware
Man-in-the-Middle (MitM) Attacks: Intercepting communications between two parties to steal or alter information

Insider Threats

Malicious or unintentional actions by individuals within an organization that compromise security.

Data breaches

Unauthorized access to sensitive information.
