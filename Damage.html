DAMAGES

Financial Loss

Theft of money, fraud, and the cost of recovering data or systems.

Data Breaches

Exposure of sensitive personal or organizational data

Reputation Damage

Loss of trust from customers, stakeholders, or the public

Operational Disruption

Interruptions to business processes, services, or critical systems.

Legal Consequences

Fines, lawsuits, or penalties for non-compliance with data protection laws.

Identity Theft

Unauthorized use of personal information for fraudulent purposes.

Loss of Intellectual Property

Theft of proprietary or confidential business information.

Damage to Infrastructure

Disruption of critical infrastructure, such as utilities or healthcare systems.

Loss of Customer Base

Customers may leave due to a lack of trust in security.

National Security Threats

Compromises to government systems or defense networks.
