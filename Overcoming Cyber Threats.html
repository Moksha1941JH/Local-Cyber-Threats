DOGDGING CYBER BULLETS

Cyber attacks are no longer a foreign concept to our fellow Sri Lankans. Therefore, it's vital to know how and when to dodge these attacks skillfully.

This topic can be discussed under five main categories.

    1.Prevention: Building Strong Defenses

    Keep Software Updated

    Regularly update operating systems, software, and security patches to fix vulnerabilities.

    Use Strong Passwords

    Create unique, complex passwords and use password managers to secure them.

    Install Antivirus and Anti-Malware Software

    Use trusted tools to detect and remove malicious software.

    Educate and Train Users

    Intercepting communications between two parties to steal or alter information

    Restrict Access

    Grant access to sensitive data only to those who need it.

    Data breaches

    Unauthorized access to sensitive information.

    2.Detection: Recognizing Attacks Early

    Monitor Network Activity

    Use tools to track unusual activities or unauthorized access attempts.

    Set Up Alerts

    Configure systems to alert you to suspicious behavior, such as multiple failed login attempts.

    3.Response: Acting Quickly

    Activate an Incident Response Plan

    Have a pre-defined plan that outlines steps to contain and manage the attacks in communities.

    Isolate Affected Systems

    Disconnect compromised devices from the network to prevent the spread of the attackn

    Notify Stakeholders

    Inform your team, customers, and authorities about the breach if sensitive data is compromised.

    Engage Experts

    Work with cybersecurity professionals to assess and mitigate the damage

    4.Recovery: Rebuilding Safely

    Clean Compromised Systems: Use trusted tools to remove malware or restore systems to a clean state.

    Enhance Security Post-Attack

    Identify weaknesses exploited by attackers and strengthen your defenses.

    5.Long-Term Strategies

    Implement Zero-Trust Architecture

    Assume no user or device is trustworthy by default, requiring constant verification

    Conduct Penetration Testing

    Regularly test your systems to find and fix weaknesses.

    Stay Informed

    Keep up with the latest cybersecurity trends and threats to stay ahead of attackers.
